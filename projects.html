Class Portal

I challenged myself to design and develop a class portal to consolidate all assignments, projects, and documentation in one place. The portal is be a good representation of my current design and web development skills in vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS. It has a large landing page and seperate pages for both assignments and projects, and uses flex boxes to establish layout while keeping the site responsive. This site is the portal!
